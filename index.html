    服務說明與使用限制

    關於本服務

    本服務依據使用者自行填寫的年齡、生活型態、家族史、既往病史及其他健康資訊，運用統計與人工智慧方法，提供癌症相關風險因子的個人化整理與健康教育資訊。

    本結果不代表罹患癌症的機率，不用於癌症診斷、篩檢、早期偵測、疾病預測或治療決策，亦不能取代醫師評估或任何標準醫療檢查。

    模型現況與限制

    本服務使用內部概念驗證階段的統計與人工智慧模型。結果會受自行填寫資料的完整性、正確性、研究資料與模型方法限制影響，且仍需持續驗證與優化。

    研究驗證指標

    ROC-AUC 用於評估模型在研究資料中進行排序與區分的能力，不是個人預測正確率。敏感度反映模型在特定研究資料與判定設定下降低遺漏個案的能力，不能用於確認或排除個人是否罹患疾病。

    本平台包含 37 項評估項目，資料結構化後對應 58 項模型特徵，用於產生癌症相關健康風險因子的個人化整理。

    如有持續或明顯身體不適，請尋求合格醫療人員評估。

    About this service

    This service uses statistical and artificial intelligence methods based on age, lifestyle, family history, past medical history, and other health information self-reported by the user to provide personalized organization of cancer-related risk factors and health education information.

    The result does not represent the probability of developing cancer and is not intended for cancer diagnosis, screening, early detection, disease prediction, or treatment decision-making. It also cannot replace a physician's evaluation or any standard medical examination.

    Current model status and limitations

    This service uses statistical and artificial intelligence models at an internal proof-of-concept stage. Results may be affected by the completeness and accuracy of self-reported information, the research data, and methodological limitations. The models require continued validation and improvement.

    Research validation metrics

    ROC-AUC evaluates the model's ability to rank and distinguish groups within the research data; it is not an individual's prediction accuracy. Sensitivity reflects the model's ability to reduce missed cases under a particular research dataset and decision setting and cannot confirm or rule out whether an individual has a disease.

    The platform includes 37 assessment items. After structuring, the collected information maps to 58 model features used to produce a personalized organization of cancer-related health risk factors.
